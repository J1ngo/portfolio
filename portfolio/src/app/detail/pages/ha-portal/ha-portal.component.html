Health Ally Provider Portal

As part of my work for a startup called OSIA Medical, I ideated, designed, developed, and implemented a Provider (Doctors, Nurses, Staff, etc.) Portal, where said providers could review and track their patients through our platform.

The site was developed in AngularJS (v. 1.3.x) and used OSIA's underlying platform and a RESTful API to transmit patient data to and fro. The general idea is that patients with supported chronic conditions provided longitudinal data via the Health Ally mobile app, and providers received this data via our web portal. Additionally, providers have the ability to message their patients directly via a secure messaging client within the portal (and the app).

Another key feature was the "Triage" portion of the dashboard, which highlighted chronic patients who reported abnormal values as part of the main workflow. This was done to both lower the risk of emergency room visits and to improve scheduling; after all, the patients who benefit the most from the direct attention of doctors are those currently in the throes of their condition.
