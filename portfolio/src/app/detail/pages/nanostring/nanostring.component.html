Nanostring

Nanostring provides instruments and services for biological research, including immuno-oncology and (soon) neuroscience. I'm currently working on the R&D team with them to develop their new Digital Spatial Profiler (DSP) device, which images targeted cell samples in ultra-high definition and provides multiplexed analysis of said samples.

As it is an unreleased final product, I actually can't share screenshots for visuals. The most I can do is point you to their website for the product: https://www.nanostring.com/scientific-content/technology-overview/digital-spatial-profiling-technology.

As a senior engineer, my role has been to develop the web application and its corresponding services and databases.

Specifically, we've built the web app in Angular 6.x, the services in .NET core 2.0, the database in Postgres, all of this running in a tangled web of isolated Docker containers.
