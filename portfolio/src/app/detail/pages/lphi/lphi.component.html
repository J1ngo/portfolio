LPHI (Louisiana Patient Health Index)

As part of our first all-hands-on deck endeavor (since the merger), HarmonIQ's Salt Lake and Louisiana divisions are currently working on a large-scale project for hospitals in Louisiana.

The project is large in scope, but the initial impetus for is was a need to archive terabytes of patient health records from several different health systems. So far, my work on the project has been to build the frontend for clinical admins to merge patient records that our algorithms have determined have a high likelihood of describing the same patient. We succeeded in building that tool.
